battery stats methods:

BATTERY INFO
$ upower -i `upower -e | grep 'BAT'` 
finds device battery and displays some info
filtering multiple stats:
upower -i /org/freedesktop/UPower/devices/battery_BAT0| grep -E "state|to\ full|percentage"'
dis/charge rate:
